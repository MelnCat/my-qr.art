What do I do?

On the top of the page is your custom QR code. Try to scan it, share it with your friends, or save it to your computer, by left clicking on the QR code and choosing 'Save image as' (or equivalent). On phones it might work a bit differently. In worst case scenario, simply take a screenshot of the code.

Where does my QR code go?

Your QR code leads to {{ qr_url }}. If you scan the QR code you should be redirected to there.

Can I also have it in vector formats?

Of course! You can get your QR code in SVG format here or in EPS format here. You might need to right click these images and select "Save link as" to save them to your computer. If you need any other format, you can probably use any online converter from the SVG format to whatever you want!
